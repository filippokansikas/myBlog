Who is Filippo Kansikas

My name is Filippo Kansikas, and I've been actively involved in the crypto space for the past three years as an investment consultant. My experience spans working with early-stage DeFi projects, advising on strategic growth, and helping teams navigate the complexities of decentralized finance. Beyond consulting, I've played a key role in developing tokenomics models for multiple projects, ensuring sustainable economic frameworks. I was also involved in the launch of a cryptocurrency project via an IEO on a widely recognized exchange. Additionally, I collaborated with a business on the creation of a copy trading product, bridging automation with smart investment strategies. Outside of my professional work, I enjoy swimming and actively engaging with my personal development community, which I built using a Threads social media page. This platform allows me to connect with like-minded individuals, sharing insights on growth, mindset, and success. I'm excited to be here today and look forward to exchanging ideas with all of you.
